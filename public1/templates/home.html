In life, nothing is to be feared, everything is to be understood. Marie Curie

By transforming mystery into challenge, science adds a new dimension to life. And a new dimension opens several paths to self-fulfillment.

Science and technology also aim to develop interest in scientific and technical progress. This is why our educational schemes are aligned with an investigative approach that fosters curiosity and creativity.

It is not in the object that lies the meaning of things, but in the process. Saint-Exupéry

Art undoubtedly occupies a special place in the heart of contemporary society, in which the relation to art pieces and creation is transformed by the development of media.

Spectators, creators or interpreters, all of our students are encouraged to develop an ability to give meaning to art and to perceive in it human, social and economic challenges, in order to open up to others.

What are books good for, if they don’t make us feel alive, if they can’t make us live it more intensely? Henry Miller

Beyond a concept of reading and besides contributing to the acquisition of language proficiency, literature also engages the student’s vision.

We are convinced that literature opens the mind, raises awareness to humanity, helps to see with the heart and is essential to any kind of responsible political life in the current context of globalization.

Sport seeks out fear to dominate it, exhaustion to triumph over it, difficulty to overcome it. Pierre de Coubertin

Physical education allows students to develop a sense of effort, risk-taking and perseverance. By putting into action moral and social core values, such as respect for collective rules or respect for self and others, it provides a favorable context for teaching responsibility and autonomy.

This is why our sports teams continue to radiate the image of our institution in different environments and countries!

Real generosity towards the future lies in giving all to the present. Albert Camus

Each year, we welcome in the Lycée several children from families in financial difficulty, who are sometimes subsidized bythe French state, primarily for school fees. We set aside a social envelope for disadvantaged students to help them to continue to attend school and various additional educational projects, such assports trips.

Fully respecting our equal opportunity policy, our social envelope is the embodiment of our commitment to deliver excellence for all.
